AIAS.

Hail, offspring of the Highest! Pallas, hail! Well hast thou stood by me. Triumphal gold Shall crown thy temple for this lordly prey.

ATH.

A fair intention! But resolve me this: Hast dyed thy falchion deep in Argive blood?

AI.

There is my boast; that charge I’ll ne’er deny.

ATH.

Have Atreus’ sons felt thy victorious might?

AI.

I take it, then, they are dead.
